
## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes Blinkit's retail sales data to uncover valuable business insights using Excel, Python, SQL, and Power BI. The dashboard provides an interactive view of sales performance, outlet efficiency, product categories, customer preferences, and overall business trends to support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

The primary objective of this project is to analyze Blinkit's retail sales data and identify meaningful business insights by evaluating:

- Sales performance
- Product category contribution
- Outlet performance
- Customer preferences
- Sales trends
- Store distribution

## 🧹 Data Preparation

The dataset was cleaned and transformed before visualization.

- Handled missing values
- Removed inconsistencies in categorical values
- Standardized column names
- Rounded numerical values
- Verified duplicate records
- Imported cleaned data into SQL Server using SQLAlchemy

## 📈 Key Business Insights

- Low Fat products contribute nearly **65%** of total sales and orders.
- Fruits & Vegetables and Snack Foods are the highest revenue-generating categories.
- Tier 3 outlets generate the highest overall sales.
- Medium-sized outlets outperform other outlet sizes.
- Supermarket Type1 contributes the largest share of total sales and orders.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- Expand the Low Fat product portfolio.
- Prioritize inventory for high-performing product categories.
- Increase investment in Tier 3 markets.
- Focus future expansion on medium-sized outlets.
- Replicate the operational strategy of Supermarket Type1 stores.
